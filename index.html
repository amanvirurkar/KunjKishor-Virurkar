About Me

I am Kunj Kishor Virurkar, an experienced educationist, administrator, and motivational speaker with over 25 years of leadership experience as the Principal of a renowned English medium school in Balaghat. Throughout my career, I have been dedicated to fostering academic excellence, holistic development, and value-based education. My expertise extends beyond administration — I am also a skilled speaker, parenting expert, and motivator, committed to guiding individuals and families toward personal growth and success. In addition to my role in education, I am a writer, poet, and announcer at All India Radio, where I use the power of words to inspire and connect with people. My background as a programmer further reflects my interest in technology and innovation, which I believe are essential elements in modern education. Above all, I consider myself a lifelong learner and a compassionate human being, dedicated to creating positive change through knowledge, creativity, and empathy.
